Yes. Kalinga Traders discusses dispatch for bulk buyers across India based on quantity, route, packing, and availability.

Minimum order quantity depends on product grade, packing, and destination. Share your requirement in MT for a practical quote discussion.

Yes. Feed grade maize, food grade maize, yellow maize, and bulk maize supply are listed product categories.

Can moisture level be discussed before dispatch?

Yes. Moisture, purity, foreign matter, broken grains, and packaging expectations can be aligned before order confirmation.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on grade, season, lot availability, quantity, packing, loading format, and delivery location.
